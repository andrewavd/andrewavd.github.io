About

I am a curious Software Engineer, I've always been intrigued by the how, what, when and why of “things”.

That curiosity and intrigue has fueled my imagination and propelled my desire to understand. I'm a strong proponent of: "The perfect solution lies in the perfect understanding." To that end, I'm always looking for ways to better understand, so that I may better solve.

Whether it be training for and running marathons, "going back" to school, providing rideshares or solving problems, I love to experience the things that push my boundaries and provide me with new experiences and insights. My individual efforts have been fruitful, but it all has had its genesis in the wisdom, support and encouragement of those around me.

Be good, and we'll see you, "out there"!
